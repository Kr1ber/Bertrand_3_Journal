The five different types of Java data types are:

1) int-Is an integer,i.e, a "counting number", e.g., 42

2) float-A floating point decimal, 1.4635897/ 3.14159

3) short,long,double-These are other numbers w/ decimals.

4) string-A LOT of characters, e.g, "h8e;430'"

5) boolean-True or False

6) char-Just ONE character, e.g., "1" "A" "P" "Q"(Just in case)
